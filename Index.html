Sahara Desert
Desert in Africa

The Sahara is a desert on the African continent with an area of 9.2million square kilometres,it is the largest hot desert in the world and the third-largest desert overall.It is one of the driest and hottest regions of the world, with a mean temperatures sometimes over 30 degrees celsius and avg high temperature in summer are over 40 degrees celsius for months at a time and soar to 47 degrees celsius.Snow has been recorded several times in the Sahara Desert over the last decades,most recently in January 2022,it may be unusual but it is not unprecedented in the region
